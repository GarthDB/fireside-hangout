What is The Fireside Hangout?

We're a group of designers and developers who met at Blend Conference and, while the speaker sessions were amazing, we spent the majority of our free time around a table diving into talks on design theory, new programming languages, and things we found intriguing and amusing.

These monthly, live Hangouts are a continuation of those discussions.
